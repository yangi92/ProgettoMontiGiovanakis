Business & Market

Our strategic markets are worldwide. Operations are conducted by dedicated Business Units: the Domestic Business Unit, which includes fixed and mobile voice and data services for retail and wholesale operators as well as international wholesale services (TIM International) an IT solutions, and the Abroad Business Unit.

Our organisation, in line with the evolution of the sector business model and market and technological trends, is shifting towards a "Digital Tecl & Platform Company" model based on innovative infrastructure and an excellent costumer service, increasingly aimed at disseminating premium services and digital content within a customizable platform, accessible anywhere and on any device.

Commercial management of the retail costumers-namely private (individual consumers and families) and business costumers , is based on these organizational drivers:

-guaranteeing the end to end accountability of the marketing, sales and post-sales processes for each costumer segment.

-encouraging the development of integrated "costumer centric" offers and the focus on digital market services.

-creating synergies between commercial channels to increase the efficiency and effectiveness of the multichannel model

-encouraging the development of "platform based" services expanding TIM's offer portofolio.

-paying attention to mulinational costumers in order to develop international connectivity

For this purpose , activities on the domestic market are structued into different deparments.
